Understanding Migraines

Educational resources about migraine conditions

What is a Migraine?

A migraine is a neurological condition characterized by intense, debilitating headaches. Symptoms often include throbbing pain (usually on one side), nausea, sensitivity to light and sound, and can last from 4 to 72 hours.

Common Triggers

  • Stress and anxiety
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Hormonal changes
  • Weather changes
  • Certain foods & drinks
  • Sensory overload

Migraine Phases

  • Prodrome (warning signs)
  • Aura (sensory disturbances)
  • Headache (pain phase)
  • Postdrome (recovery)

Prevention Tips

  • Maintain regular sleep
  • Stay hydrated
  • Manage stress levels
  • Track your triggers
  • Regular exercise
