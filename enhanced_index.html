📚 Understanding Migraines

🧠 What is a Migraine?

A migraine is a neurological condition causing intense, throbbing headaches often on one side of the head. It can last 4-72 hours and significantly impact daily activities.

⚡ Common Triggers

  • Stress and anxiety
  • Sleep changes
  • Hormonal fluctuations
  • Weather changes
  • Certain foods & drinks
  • Sensory overload
